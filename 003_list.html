How To Cook My Favourite Meal


Egusi soup


Ingredients

  • blended egusi - 1-2 cups<./li>
  • palm oil - half cup<./li>
  • protein of choice.
  • crayfish - 2 tablespoons.
  • seasoning cubes -3.
  • salt - to taste.
  • pepper -blended.
  • washed or chopped bitterleaf or spinach.
  • onions - 1 medium size.
  • water or protein stock as needed.

Steps

  1. heat palm oil in a pot without bleaching it.
  2. add the blended pepper and onions and fry for a few minutes.
  3. stir in the egusi and fry until it start forming lumps<./li>
  4. gradually add the stock or water and stir well.
  5. put in your protein.
  6. season with your crayfish, cubes and salt.
  7. allow to cook until the oil floats ontop.
  8. add the vegetables and shimmer for a few minutes.
  9. stir and serve while still hot.

Summary

 Egusi soup is one of the most popular soups in nigeria.

 It is easy to prepare with the right ingredients.

 It goes well with any kind of swallow and also with rice.
